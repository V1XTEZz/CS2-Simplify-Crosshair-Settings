# CS2-Simplify-Crosshair-Settings
This CFG will help you to customize ur crosshair in CS2.

<p>Download the config and put it: <blockquote>...\steamapps\common\Counter-Strike Global Offensive\game\csgo\cfg</blockquote>
<br>Next U can bind it or copy the [Alias SCS] to ur config and after just write in console SCS.
<br>To control Alpha & RGB download Easy RGBA > https://github.com/V1XTEZz/CS2-Easy-Control-RGBA-Crosshair</p>

<br><b>The Settings</b>:
<pre><i>CGap[num]</i> - <code>(0 to -5) Gap between crosshair's lines</code>
<br><i>CSize[num]</i> - <code>(0 / 0.5 to 10) Size of crosshair</code>
<br><i>CThickness[num]</i> / <i>CThicc[num]</i> - <code>(1 or 2) Thickness/width of crosshair</code>
<br><i>CStyle[num]</i> - (1 to 5) Changing style of crosshair, 
<i>1 (old crosshair) 2(double crosshair) 3(classic dynamic) 4 (static) 5(shots dynamic only)</i>
<br><i>CDot[num]</i> - <code>(0 or 1)turning off/on dot for crosshair</code>
<br><i>CT[num]</i> - <code>(0 or 1)turning off/on T style crosshair</code>
<br><i>Outline[num]</i> - <code>(0 or 1) turning off/on outline</code>
<br><i>CFF[num]</i> - <code>(0 or 1) Frendly warning</code>
<br><i>CGapUWV[num]</i> - <code>(0 or 1) Use Weapon Value</code>
<br><i>CCol[num]</i> - <code>(0 to 5) Colors 0(red) 1(green) 2(yellow) 3(blue) 4(cyan) 5(custom)</code>

<br><i>SCSize[num]</i> - <code>(1 or 2) Sniper crosshair thickness(width)</code></pre>
